Top Three Activities to do in Bath

Visit the Roman Baths

The Roman Baths are a historic site in Bath, England, that date back to the Roman Empire. They are located at the heart of the city and are known for their thermal waters, which have been used for bathing and healing since ancient times.

Visit the Bath Abbey

Bath Abbey is a parish church of the Church of England and a former Benedictine monastery. Founded in the 7th century, the current building dates from the 16th century and is known for its fan vaulting, stained glass windows, and rich history.

Visit the Jane Austen Centre

Jane Austen Centre offers a snapshot of life during Regency times and explores how living in Bath affected Jane Austen's life and writing. The centre features a permanent exhibition and knowledgeable guides dressed in period costume.
